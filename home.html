Frequently Asked Questions

Influence refers to the ability to affect decisions, opinions, or actions of others.

An influencer is an individual who can affect the purchase decisions of others because of their authority, knowledge, or relationship with their audience.

Influencers are typically authentic, engaging, and knowledgeable within a particular niche or industry.

Influencers can help brands reach a wider audience and build trust through their existing relationships with their followers.

Influencers primarily use social media platforms like Instagram, YouTube, Twitter, TikTok, and LinkedIn to engage with their audiences.

Influencers are typically paid through sponsored content, brand partnerships, affiliate marketing, or ad revenue from platforms like YouTube.
